About

What does T.D.Mac Consulting provide?

Current services include Web Development and Digital Development Consultations. This includes static brochure sites for businesses looking to create or improve upon their current online presence. As T.D.Mac Consulting grows and its skillsets widen, more advanced digital services will be available.

Is T.D.Mac Consulting right for your business?

There are many businesses and sole traders with only a social media presence and no website. Others may have a self-built websites that lack performance, or have limited functionality based on their current website provider, ultimately restricting the growth of their business. Those small businesses and sole traders are often looking to expand their business potential and explore new, more effective ways, to interact and trade with their customers.

If you are one of those small businesses or sole traders, then the services provided by T.D.Mac Consulting will aim to deliver you an increase in business potential by optimising the online customer experience.

What is the process?

Consultation and Report

Through consulation we will explore how your business operates and ways you are looking to improve your business with digital solutions. Following the initial consultation, a small report will be generated recommending the various options available for your business to improve operational processes and your customer's experience.

Quotation and Specification

Once a solution has been agreed upon, the planning process will begin and a quotation including full specification will be generated for review. As part of this process any financial considerations can be factored into the quote including a modular delivery timeline and payment scheme.

Development and Review

If an agreement is made on the specifcation of work to proceed then the real work begins. The development stages will provide ample opportunity for regular reviews throughout the process with full transparency regarding the progress of the work. This will mean that the finished project is bespoke and meets all the specified outcomes, mitigating any risk of misguided project direction, or a sub-optimal final product.

Testing and Deployment

Before the project is signed off, testing of the product will be undertaken and a soft launch will take place where a bug fixing window will be observed. After this a final review process will take place and the product will be launched completely for the benefit of your business and your customers.

Product Support

After the launch of your new digital solutions, full support will be available, ensuring you are never left without the help you might need.
